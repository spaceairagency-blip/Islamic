Pilgrimage Guide

Step-by-step rituals for Hajj and Umrah

1

Ihram

Enter the state of purity at the Meeqat. Perform Ghusl, wear the Ihram garments, and make the intention (Niyyah).

2

Tawaf

Circle the Kaaba seven times anti-clockwise, starting from the Hajar al-Aswad (Black Stone).

3

Saâ€™i

Walk seven times between the hills of Safa and Marwah, starting at Safa.

4

Halq or Taqsir

Shaving the head (Halq) or trimming the hair (Taqsir) to complete the Umrah and exit Ihram.

1

8th Dhul Hijjah

Put on Ihram and head to Mina. Stay there and perform the five daily prayers starting from Dhuhr.

2

9th Dhul Hijjah (Arafat)

The core of Hajj. Travel to Arafat after Fajr. After sunset, move to Muzdalifah to spend the night.

3

10th Dhul Hijjah (Eid day)

Perform Rami (stoning Jamrat al-Aqaba), sacrifice an animal, shave hair, and perform Tawaf al-Ifadah.

4

11th-13th Dhul Hijjah

Stay in Mina for the stoning of the three pillars (Jamarat) and perform Farewell Tawaf before leaving Makkah.
